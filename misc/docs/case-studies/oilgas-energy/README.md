# Oil & Gas Analytics Demo: Production Operations

> **Synthetic Data Demonstration** - All reports use synthetic example data for portfolio demonstration purposes. Metrics and insights are illustrative and do not represent any real company.

---

## Overview

This case study demonstrates enterprise-grade oil & gas production analytics for a fictional upstream operator. The analysis covers 500,000 production records across multiple basins with full operational and financial KPIs.

---

## Industry Context

**Sector**: Oil & Gas / Upstream Production
**Data Volume**: 500,000 records
**Analysis Period**: 2023-2026 (3 years)
**Regions**: Permian Basin, Eagle Ford, Bakken, DJ Basin

## Industry Standards

KPIs computed using standard oil & gas methodologies:
- BOE conversion: 6:1 MCF to BBL ratio
- Netback: Revenue minus royalties, transport, operating costs
- Lifting cost: Operating expenses per BOE produced
