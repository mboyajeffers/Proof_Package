# iGaming Analytics Demo: Online Casino Platform

> **Synthetic Data Demonstration** - All reports use synthetic example data for portfolio demonstration purposes. Metrics and insights are illustrative and do not represent any real company.

---

## Overview

This case study demonstrates enterprise-grade iGaming analytics for a fictional online casino platform ("LuckySpin"). The analysis covers 400,000 gaming events with a dual focus on business performance and responsible gaming compliance.

---

## Industry Context

**Sector**: iGaming / Online Casino
**Data Volume**: 400,000 events
**Analysis Period**: FY 2025
**Markets**: UK, Germany, Canada, Sweden, Finland

## Key Metrics Demonstrated

### Gaming Performance
- Total Wagered: $4.26M
- Gross Gaming Revenue: $128K
- Hold Percentage: 3.0%
- Active Players: 115,690

### Channel Distribution
- Mobile: 74% of events (A+ sustainability grade)
- Web: 25% of events (A sustainability grade)
- Retail: 1% of events (B sustainability grade)

### Game Performance
- Slots: 60.4% of wagering, 1.96% hold
- Roulette: 9.5% of wagering, 1.35% hold
- Live Roulette: 9.3% of wagering, 1.95% hold
- Blackjack: 7.0% of wagering, 0.21% hold

### Responsible Gaming Metrics
- RG Flag Rate: 5.40% (17% better than benchmark)
- Bonus Abuse Rate: 0.54% (73% better than benchmark)
- Velocity Score: 19.0 (24% better than benchmark)
