# E-Commerce Analytics Demo: Luxury Jewelry

> **Synthetic Data Demonstration** - All reports use synthetic example data for portfolio demonstration purposes. Metrics and insights are illustrative and do not represent any real company.

---

## Overview

This case study demonstrates enterprise-grade e-commerce analytics for a fictional luxury jewelry retailer ("Luxe Gemstone Co."). The analysis covers 800,000 transactions across multiple product categories, customer segments, and sales channels.

---

## Industry Context

**Sector**: Luxury Retail / E-Commerce
**Data Volume**: 800,000 transactions
**Analysis Period**: FY 2025
**Markets**: US, UK, EU, Australia

## Key Metrics Demonstrated

### Revenue & Orders
- Total Revenue: $9.48B
- Total Orders: 800,000
- Average Order Value: $11,845
- Unique Customers: 119,533

### Customer Segmentation
- VIP Elite: 5% of customers, $18,495 AOV
- Premium: 15% of customers, $13,648 AOV
- Regular: 40% of customers, $10,275 AOV
- Occasional: 25% of customers, $9,379 AOV
- New: 15% of customers, $9,629 AOV

### Category Performance
- Engagement Rings: 37.4% of revenue
- Fine Watches: 24.5% of revenue
- Necklaces: 11.1% of revenue
- Earrings: 8.7% of revenue
